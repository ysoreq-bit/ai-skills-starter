## Adoption rules

- Review sources item by item rather than importing collections wholesale.
- Match instructions to the actual environment and versions.
- Define positive and negative triggers.
- Inspect executable scripts and hooks before running them.
- Compare objective workflows against the existing baseline.
- Keep optional tools outside the core workflow until a representative pilot shows value.
- Preserve license and attribution requirements.
